Information Design

Why is it important to design the presentation of information?

  • Efficient Understanding
  • Effective Understanding
  • Improved Decision Making
  • Increased Engagement
  • Enhance Accessibility
  • Increase Navigability
  • Improve User Experience

What are the things you need to do ?

  • Organize
  • Arrange
  • Cluster
  • Prioritize
  • Guide
  • Annotate
  • Contextualize
  • Connect
  • Clarify

There are lots of fields that you need to consider in information design. They are:

  • Information Science
  • Information Technology
  • Psychology
  • Graphic Design
  • Communication
  • Computer Science
  • Social Computing
  • Data Science
  • Data Visualization

The processes involved in design process are:

  • Identify Audidence
  • Define and Communicate structure
  • Define Metadata
  • Diversify Representations
  • Emphasize
  • Simplify
  • Clarify
